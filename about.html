A bit more about me

My name is Karolina and I’m currently based in the beautiful city of Wiesbaden. As a UX/UI Designer my passion is to create visually appealing and functional designs to make users’ lives easier.

In the past 3 years I have worked for two tech companies in the SaaS industry, where I could gain a broad understanding of how modern tech companies build their software products.

Prior to transitioning my career towards UX/UI Design I worked as a Talent Acquisition Specialist, specializing in Product & Engineering. Throughout this journey I have been strongly involved in building and scaling Product, Design and Engineering teams. Working in Talent Acquisition equipped me with various crucial skills for a designer such as stakeholder management, adaptability, resillience, problem-solving, research skills or effective communication with diverse partners.

In my free time I’m always up to something creative. Music and dance are my two primary passions. From my early years, I've been devoted to dance, exploring a range of styles including ballet, ballroom, and hip hop. In parallel, my love for music has driven me to learn how to play drums. On the weekends you can usually find me attending various metal concerts and festivals.
